HOME OF ATHENA

Welcome to the Home of Athena, a reading community designed as a cozy and comfortable space to discuss literature. We welcome all reading levels, ages, and backgrounds.

Join us on Discord

HOME

The Book Buddies program allows you to form reading groups, set reading goals and complete star tasks together.

The dead poet's society is a place where you can find a group or pair to buddy read the same book together

Reading/study sessions encourage our members to read and study together. Our librarians regularly host sessions.

Earn stars for participating in reading sessions and events. You will also be rewarded stars for submitting reading time

Readathons are hosted once a month. Our readathons are usually 24 or 28 hours, and have their own themes, and challenges you can complete for stars.

Weekly writing prompts will be given to writers weekly and the winning entry will receive stars
